Project Overview

The design problem was presenting dry reasearch regarding the health benefits of dogs in a visually captivating way. Dogs for Health solves this problm by presenting data through illustrations, typography, and engaging graphs. The goal for Dogs for Health is to promote canine companionship and create positivity towards dogs of all breeds.
