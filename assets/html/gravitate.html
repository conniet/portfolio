Project Overview

Gravitate is a concept app that allows friends and family members to easily stay connected with each other at crowded venues.

Working in a team of 6, we designed the look and feel of Gravitate using space posters from the 1970s as inspiration. We extended our space theme to the website by covering the background with stars to emulate outer space, and using space themed illustrations. Each section was designed with ample whitespace to provide focus to the content, and to create a feeling of moving through space. Illustrations and text were staggered to give the website visual variety.

In addition to collaborating on the website design, I helped design the app functionality and layout.

The Problem

People usually travel in groups to large venues such as concerts, festivals, and sporting events. The problem is that large groups rarely stay together, but often plan on leaving together. When it's time to go, gathering everyone can become a time consuming pain.

Putting It Together

Mobile Application — We designed Gravitate to be a mobile only application. The problem takes place when people are out, and their mobile device is their main source of communication.

Group Communication — Gravitate is designed to provide users with the ability to easily communicate with each other. Rather than scream over a loud crowd or spend time typing out long messages riddled with typos or autocorrect errors, we've provided users with quick message options to let their group know they're stepping away.
